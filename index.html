kittenmilk
Gi perrine has been a creative trendsetter in the world of hair design for over 8 years, and is currently styling in Philadelphia.

Whether cutting or coloring, Gi  focuses on creating intuitive looks that fit each person's unique persona, without the hassles that come with overstyling.

gi is known for her explorative approach, and is always eager to try out the latest trends. So if there’s something new out there you’re eager to try, she’s the one to bring that to!

When not at the salon, Gi spends her free time watching anime, playing video games, and with her adorable pets, many of which you may know already.
